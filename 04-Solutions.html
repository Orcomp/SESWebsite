    This is the planning engine that actually constructs the schedule once the user has selected the required sequence using scheduling assistant.

    Our planning engine has an API that allows us to add custom scheduling logic to automatically perform certain scheduling rules whenever the scheduler wants to put operations on the board.

    For example in a food factory you may want it to automatically add a cleaning job every 24 hours of continuous running on some resources.

    In addition to working in conjunction with our Scheduling Assistant module, our planning engine can run completely independently (headless) on a server where it can automatically generate schedules on request.

    This means that it could be used for on-demand capable to promise applications.

    When you just want plan the right sequence of jobs on resources our Factory sequencer is the perfect tool. Often our clients have multi step production facilities, but they just want to plan the right sequence of work on a kay resource. Once that is done correctly the rest of the facility can plan their work from the sequence on the key resource.

    This occurs a lot in food manufacturing but is not limited to that environment. Often these clients have more than one resource that they plan this way. As long as you are just considering the sequence of jobs on a single resource at a time, independently of any other resources in your facility then Factory Sequencer is right for you.

    Factory sequencer has lots of functionality right off the shelf: minimising changeovers, drag and drop, sort and filter to name a few. It also has modules that have been developed for specific industry sectors like beverages and timber milling. We can also create Client specific modules that plug seamlessly into the Factory Sequencer user experience to ensure it does exactly what you need for your unique facility.
