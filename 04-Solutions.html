    Software Solutions

    WildGums have developed a suite of modules that cover a wide range of functions each in its own feature rich, easy to use environment that empowers our users to make better more informed decisions.

    We select the most appropriate combination of modules to provide our clients with the solution that best suits their needs. Other modules can be easily added when and if they’re needed

    Many WildGums products are ready to use off the shelf, however some are also able to be extended if required in order to meet very specific client needs. WildGums Planner is a great example of this.

    Each module is also application independent and this means that while we pass information between our modules, you can use each module as a stand-alone application and provide it with information from any source as long as it’s in the right format.

    As well as providing our standard modules, our professional development team can create custom built applications for our clients that are robust, quality assured and follow all the same principles we build into our own off the shelf solutions.
